## 🔄 How Does Our Turo Clone Work?

### User Registration
Users will create accounts or log in with the existing credentials, and they will be given access to the features and functionalities of the platform.

### Vehicle Listing
People put their cars on rent, leaving pictures and descriptions on their posts to increase the number of people who would want to rent their cars.

### Search and Booking
Renters filter and search by vehicles available, choose those they want, and make booking requests.

### Booking Confirmation
The owners are the ones who will go through booking requests and accept or reject the booking requests and pass the pickup information to the renters to complete the bookings.

### Rental Period
Renters collect the vehicles that have been booked at the agreed point, utilize them during the agreed time, and deliver them back.

### Payment and Review
The process is secure regarding the payment, and both renters and owners are able to leave reviews and ratings depending on their experience with the rental.
